I chose this image because the composition uses framing and symmetry to draw attention to the vending machine at the center. The machine is placed almost perfectly in the middle of the shot, creating a balanced and organized feeling even though the items inside are visually busy. The two machines on either side naturally frame the main subject and help direct the viewer’s focus. I also liked the contrast between the soft pink wall and the bright colors of the snacks, which adds visual interest and keeps the viewer’s eyes moving across the image. This mix of color, balance, and framing is why I found this composition appealing.
