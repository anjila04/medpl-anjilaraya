I chose this image because I liked the strong sense of depth created by the long, straight street leading into the distance. The buildings on both sides form natural leading lines that guide the viewer’s eye toward the center of the photo. I also liked how the symmetry of the buildings creates balance, even though the scene itself is busy. The bright pink sky adds a surprising contrast that makes the image feel more modern and eye-catching. It changes the mood of the photo and makes the city feel almost futuristic. This mix of natural lines, balance, and strong color contrast is why I found the composition interesting and chose this image.
